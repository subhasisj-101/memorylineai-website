The Problem

Why Existing EdTech
Fails Indian Students

BYJU's, Vedantu, Unacademy — they all deliver the same content to every student. One size fits none.

😴

Passive Learning

Students watch videos and read text. Nobody checks if they actually understood. They memorise for exams and forget within days.

👀

Parents Are Blind

Parents spend ₹5,000/month on tuition but have zero visibility into what their child is learning or struggling with — until exam results arrive.

📊

Teachers Work Blind

Teachers walk into class not knowing which topics confused 80% of the class last week. They teach forward into a wall of hidden confusion.

🔁

The Forgetting Problem

Students study a topic once and move on. Without spaced repetition, 70% of what they learned is forgotten within a week — guaranteed.

🤖

One Size Fits None

Every student gets the same explanation, the same pace, the same examples. A student in Bhubaneswar and a student in Mumbai are taught identically.

🎯

Memorisation Over Thinking

95% of Indian school education operates at recall level — students memorise and reproduce. Critical thinking and application are almost never developed.

Learning Science

Six Pillars of Intelligence

Every feature is grounded in decades of peer-reviewed learning science. This is not another chatbot — this is a complete learning system.

🎯

Bloom's Taxonomy Engine

Every student interaction is automatically classified into one of six thinking levels — from basic recall to creative synthesis. The AI always pushes one level higher.

Cognitive Science
🏛️

Socratic Questioning

Instead of giving answers, Vidya asks carefully designed questions that guide students to discover knowledge themselves. This creates understanding, not just memory.

Pedagogy
🧠

Ebbinghaus Spaced Repetition

Based on the 1885 forgetting curve research, concepts are automatically scheduled for review at optimal intervals — 1 day, 3 days, 7 days, 21 days, 30 days.

Memory Science
🪆

Nested Learning

Big concepts are broken into hierarchical knowledge trees. Students master small concepts before unlocking larger ones. Nobody falls behind or gets bored.

Curriculum Design
🌀

Fractal Learning

The same concept is taught at 3 levels of depth. Students revisit topics in spirals, going one level deeper each time — creating genuine mastery over weeks.

Depth Learning
🌍

Contextual Learning

Every explanation is anchored in real Indian life. Percentage calculations use local market discounts. Physics uses cricket. Chemistry uses Diwali firecrackers.
